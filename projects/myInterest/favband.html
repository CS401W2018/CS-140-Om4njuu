♥ Galdive ♥

Galdive is my favorite band of all time! Galdive is a music group from Jakarta, Indonesia. The lead singer name is Wiana, she is very talented, she can sing and write lyrics. Another person in the band is Osvaldorio. He also very talented, he had released many singles in Indonesia and outside Indonesia.

Genre of Galdive are electronic, chillout, dream pop, indietronica, indie pop, indonesian, and jazz pop. If these are your favorite genre, you should definitely check them out! You can find their music on Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, and YouTube Music.


My Top 3 Songs of Galdive:

  1. Night Charade
  2. Sweet Sugar Getaway
  3. Mundane
