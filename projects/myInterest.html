History

I have been interested in creating video games since I was a kid. My mom and my dad used to play video games together with me and my sister when I was a kid. So, I grew up love playing video games and I have a dream to make my own video games one day!~

What and Why I Love About Video Games

I love how video games can take you to a different world and let you experience things that you can't do in real life. I also love how video games can bring people together and create a community of people who share the same interests.
