Paolumu

Physiology

Paolumu is a cool monster that looks kind of like a bat, especially the Honduran white bat, which is a tiny bat with white fur and a pink face. Paolumu has white fur too, and its face is also pink! But it's not all fluffy, it has some brown scales on its back, belly, and tail. It has these funny teeth that stick out, like a beaver or a rat! That's because it loves to eat eggs from the coral. It uses its super strong breath to suck those eggs right up! If hunters want to fight it, they can hurt its head, wings, or tail to weaken it.


Behavior and Abilities

Paolumu is like a fluffy, flying monster with a super cool trick! Inside its neck, it has a special balloon-like bag that fills up with air when it breathes in really hard. Once that bag is full, Paolumu can float up in the air, just like a balloon! When it's floating, it can swoop down and attack enemies from above. It also has a strong, hard tail that it uses to whack anything that gets too close.

Paolumu can also blow really strong wind at its enemies or suck them in closer with its powerful breath. If hunters want to stop it from floating, they can climb onto its back or hit its neck to make the air bag deflate.

Paolumu is usually pretty chill and won't bother anyone unless someone bothers it first. But if it feels threatened, it gets super angry and starts fighting back.
